## How it works?
Operators receive all chats, emails and calls in one Jivo app, so they no longer have to switch between multiple windows and can be used on both mobile and computer, meaning fewer distractions and all the tools you need. for exceptional customer support.

## Is it free forever?
Yes, JivoChat has the free forever version for up to 5 operators with the features needed to make your service effective.

## So you don't have to pay anything?
Everyone who signs up has 14 days in the PRO version and can test to the fullest and know if it will really supply you and your business. If you don't make any payments after this time, your account will automatically be transferred to the FREE FOREVER version and will have fewer features.
